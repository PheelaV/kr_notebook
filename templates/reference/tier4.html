Compound Vowels

These are combinations of basic vowels. Recognition is the goal here - you'll pick these up naturally in context.

W-Vowels (Diphthongs)

Letter Sound Combination Hint
ㅘ wa ㅗ + ㅏ Like 'wa' in want
ㅝ wo ㅜ + ㅓ Like 'wo' in won
ㅙ wae ㅗ + ㅐ Like 'wa' in wax
ㅞ we ㅜ + ㅔ Like 'we' in wet
ㅟ wi ㅜ + ㅣ Like 'wee'
ㅚ oe ㅗ + ㅣ Like 'we' in wet (same as ㅙ/ㅞ)

Other Compounds

Letter Sound Combination Hint
ㅢ ui ㅡ + ㅣ Like 'oo-ee' said quickly
ㅒ yae ㅑ + ㅣ Like 'ya' in yam
ㅖ ye ㅕ + ㅣ Like 'ye' in yes

Practical Note

Many of these sound very similar or identical in modern Korean:

  • ㅙ, ㅚ, ㅞ often all sound like "we"
  • ㅒ and ㅖ often sound like "ye"

Focus on recognizing them when reading. Context will help you understand.
